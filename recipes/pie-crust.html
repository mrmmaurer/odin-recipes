Pie crust

Description

Easy, fun and fairly quick, this pie crust recipe will make a delicious base for any kind of pie!

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) all-purpose flour
  • 100g (3.5 oz) butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 8 cl (4.5 tbsp) water

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a large bowl, add butter and blend together with the tip of your fingers and palms. It should result in a sand like texture.
  2. With your hands, create a well in the middle of your mixture and pour water and salt in it.
  3. Mix and knead with your fingers, from the middle going outward, to form a ball.
  4. Let it stand for ten minutes and your pie crust will be ready to be rolled!
